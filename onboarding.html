Frequently Asked Questions

MF Utility (MFU) is an innovative \"Shared Services\" initiative by AMFI. It acts as a \"Transaction Aggregation Portal\" that allows you to transact in multiple mutual fund schemes using a single portal and a single payment.

The information is required to establish a Common Account Number (CAN). A CAN gives you a consolidated view of your investments and enables a wide range of future services and transactions without requiring repeated data entry and documentation.
